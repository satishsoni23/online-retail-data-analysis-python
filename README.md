## Online Retail Customer & Sales Analysis using Python and SQL
## 📊 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional records of an online retail store, including:

- Invoice numbers
- Product descriptions
- Quantity purchased
- Unit price
- Customer IDs
- Transaction timestamps
- Country information

This dataset simulates real-world e-commerce operations.

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Removed cancelled transactions
- Handled missing customer IDs
- Filtered invalid quantities

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Sales trends over time
- Monthly and daily revenue patterns

### 3. Product Analysis
- Top products by revenue
- Most frequently purchased items

### 4. Customer Analysis
- High-value customers
- Purchase frequency

### 5. Country-wise Analysis
- Revenue contribution by region

## 📈 Key Insights

- The United Kingdom contributed the majority of total revenue
- A small percentage of customers accounted for a large share of total sales
- Certain products like gift items and decorative goods had consistently high demand
- Sales peaked during November and December, indicating strong seasonal trends


## 📚 Key Learnings

- Importance of data cleaning before analysis
- Translating business questions into SQL queries
- Identifying key revenue drivers
- Understanding customer segmentation basics
